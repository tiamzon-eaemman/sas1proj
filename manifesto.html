Part Three

Future Manifestos

This section represents the contracts I have made with myself. It declares my purpose and commitment to holistic health. These manifestos reflect how I want to improve and define the person I aspire to be. Adhering to them will be the guide to a fruitful journey.

My Personal Manifesto

1. Discipline

I remain consistent even when progress is small. I do not fear failure; I embrace it as my necessary tutor.

2. Kindness

I show kindness to everyone. This is non-negotiable, as I believe that good energy attracts good company.

3. Ownership

I take full responsibility for my life. I focus endlessly on my growth and becoming a better version of myself.

4. Resilience

I show up, especially in tough times. When things are dark, I choose to be the light.

5. Integrity

I will be rational, even when I am afraid. I choose the hard right over the easy wrong.

Health Manifesto

1. Sleep

I prioritize sleep for longevity, never viewing recovery as a sign of exhaustion.

2. Temple

My body is a temple. My food is the foundation for immunity and a roof against disease.

3. Fuel

Whole foods give me energy and clarity; they are information for my body and mind.

4. Sweat

Intense workouts are my sign of strength. I remove mental stress and problems like a sweat.

5. Habit

I rely on habit, not motivation. Consistency is my advantage, even when progress is slow.
