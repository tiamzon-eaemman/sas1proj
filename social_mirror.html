Part Two

The Unfiltered View

This interview was conducted with a college friend and my best friend. It answers the question of how I am perceived by others. It gives their perspective on how they see me and what kind of person I am. This is not just a simple interview; it explores their experiences and observations, allowing me to know myself better.

After talking to two people who know me pretty well, I realized that their impressions of me actually line up in a lot of ways. Both of them said that I usually look serious or quiet at first, which can make me seem a bit “masungit” to people who don't know me yet. But once they actually spend time with me, that first impression disappears.

For Zielo, she said I'm actually warm, playful, and someone who's fun to be around. She mentioned that I like to tease people, but in a way that makes things lighter, not annoying. She also sees me as disciplined and hardworking—someone who really tries to give her best in what she does—but still knows how and when to enjoy. For her, I'm the type of person who grows on you the more you get to know me.

On the other hand, Djay described me as someone with a light and friendly aura. According to her, I leave a good impression because I'm easy to talk to and easy to trust. She also said I'm hardworking and not afraid to take risks, especially when it comes to getting things done. She pointed out my quirky side too—how I can be very vocal about the things I'm interested in and how my facial expressions make it obvious what mood I'm in. For her, I'm fun to hang out with because I actually listen and pay attention.

Putting both interviews together, they see me as someone who might seem quiet at first but is actually friendly, warm, and hardworking. I'm easygoing, expressive, and someone people feel comfortable with once the conversation starts.
